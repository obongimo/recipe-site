Chocolate Cake Recipe

"Here is a fast an easy version of the classic Chocolate cake recipe. Made from locally refined cocoa in Ghana. The kids will love this one. Enjoy!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10mins 25mins 35mins

Ingredients

Black Chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and the Flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

 Bake in the oven

5

 Store in Fridge
